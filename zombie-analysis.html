ZOMBIE LEVELS

LEVEL 1

KAREN ZOMBIE

Appearance: Bob hair cut, shirt with skinny jeans, do not carry weapons

What they are after: Better discount and prices, Famous quotes: “Where is your manager!”

Attributes: Weakest zombie, do not attack people but will annoy the hell out of you. Disturbs and prevents you from collecting survival items.

Ways to defeat: shoot once with survival tools

LEVEL 2

'KIASU' ZOMBIE

Appearance: typical singaporean clothes, no weapons.

What are they after: will attack you because they hate that they lost their humanity and you did not (they hate ‘losing’)

Attributes: mid-strong zombie, deals the least damage.

Ways to defeat: shoot twice with survival tools

LEVEL 3

ASIAN PARENT ZOMBIE

Appearance: usually women/mothers, carry slippers as a weapon

What are they after: will attack you to know if ur a doctor or lawyer

Attributes: Strongest zombie, deal highest damage with her slipper

Ways to defeat: shoot thrice with survival tools (unless you are a doctor)
