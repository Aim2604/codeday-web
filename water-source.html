WATER SOURCE

NATURAL SPRINGS

Look for areas where water naturally bubbles up from the ground. Clear water emerging from the ground is generally safe, but you should still purify it if possible

UNDERGROUND WATER

Dig a hole in the ground to access underground water. Wait for the hole to fill with water, and then use a container to collect it.

WATER PURIFICATION

Boil water for at least one minute to kill bacteria and parasites. Use water purification tablets or filters if available.
